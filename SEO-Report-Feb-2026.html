4. Recommendations for Future Growth

Google Business Profile (GBP) Optimization

Your Google Business Profile is critical for local search visibility. Recommended actions:

  • Add photos of completed projects regularly (aim for 5-10 new photos monthly)
  • Post weekly updates about services, tips, or completed jobs
  • Respond to all reviews promptly (within 24-48 hours)
  • Add all services with descriptions to your GBP services section
  • Ensure NAP (Name, Address, Phone) matches website exactly
Impact: Improved visibility in Google Maps and local pack results.

Backlink Building Opportunities

Quality backlinks from relevant websites improve domain authority. Strategies to pursue:

  • Local partnerships: Real estate agents, property managers, insurance agents
  • Supplier/manufacturer links: Request listing on supplier websites as an authorized installer
  • Local sponsorships: Community events, youth sports, local charities
  • Chamber of Commerce: Join local chambers for directory listings
  • Industry associations: NRCA, VSI, or local contractor associations
  • Guest posting: Contribute articles to local news sites or home improvement blogs
Impact: Increased domain authority and higher rankings for competitive keywords.

Review Generation Strategy

Online reviews directly impact local rankings and conversion rates:

  • Implement a systematic follow-up process after each completed job
  • Send review request emails with direct links to Google and Facebook
  • Goal: 2-4 new reviews per month minimum
  • Respond professionally to all reviews (positive and negative)
Impact: Higher conversion rates and improved local pack rankings.
