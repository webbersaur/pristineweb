Benefits of Adding a Deck to Your Home

A well-designed deck offers more than just outdoor living space. Here's why Connecticut homeowners invest in quality decks:

  • Increased home value – Decks offer one of the best returns on investment for home improvement projects
  • Extended living space – Create an outdoor room for dining, entertaining, or relaxing
  • Enhanced curb appeal – A beautiful deck makes your home more attractive to visitors and potential buyers
  • Connection with nature – Enjoy your backyard in comfort with a dedicated outdoor space
  • Versatile entertaining – Host barbecues, parties, and family gatherings in style

Choosing the Right Deck Material

Selecting the right decking material depends on your budget, maintenance preferences, and aesthetic goals:

Pressure-Treated Wood

The most affordable option, pressure-treated lumber is durable and widely available. It requires regular staining and sealing to maintain its appearance and protect against the elements.

Cedar and Redwood

Natural wood options that offer beautiful grain patterns and natural resistance to rot and insects. These premium woods age gracefully and can be left natural or stained.

Composite Decking

Made from a blend of wood fibers and plastic, composite decking offers the look of wood with minimal maintenance. It won't splinter, rot, or require staining, making it ideal for busy homeowners.

PVC Decking

100% synthetic material that's completely moisture-resistant. PVC decking is the lowest maintenance option and works well in high-moisture areas.
