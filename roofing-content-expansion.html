Signs Your Roof Needs Attention

Your roof protects everything beneath it. Watch for these warning signs that indicate it's time for inspection or repair:

  • Missing or damaged shingles – Exposes your roof deck to water damage
  • Granules in gutters – Indicates shingles are wearing out
  • Daylight through roof boards – Visible light in your attic means gaps exist
  • Sagging roof deck – Sign of structural damage requiring immediate attention
  • Higher energy bills – Poor roof insulation lets conditioned air escape
  • Water stains on ceilings – Active leaks need prompt repair
  • Roof age over 20 years – Most asphalt shingles need replacement after two decades
